Pain-specific validation page

Late payment problems usually start with weak contract terms.

Some clients are bad actors. But a lot of freelancers get burned because the agreement gives them no deposit, no milestone leverage, no late-fee language, and no pause-right when invoices go overdue.

5 payment clauses worth checking

  • Deposit: is there upfront commitment before work begins?
  • Milestones: are payment points tied to real deliverables?
  • Net terms: is payment due in 7, 14, or 30 days — and from what trigger?
  • Late fees: does the contract discourage dragging invoices out?
  • Work pause right: can you stop work if invoices go unpaid?

Practical wording to ask for

"A 50% deposit is due before work begins. Remaining invoices are due within 7 days of issue. Contractor may pause work on overdue accounts until payment is received."
