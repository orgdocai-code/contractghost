# ContractGhost Manual Outreach Backup

## Purpose
Keep validation moving even if no public URL is available.

## Core message
Freelancers get burned by contracts before the work even starts: scope creep, payment traps, IP grabs, vague revision language, and one-sided termination. ContractGhost is a pre-sign risk check that explains dangerous clauses in plain English.

## No-link CTA
Use this when hosting is blocked:
- "I'm testing a tool that flags risky freelancer contract terms before you sign. If you want, I can send a screenshot/demo and add you to the beta list manually."

## What to collect in every conversation
- What kind of freelance work they do
- Worst contract clause or client term they've dealt with
- Whether they currently review contracts themselves / with ChatGPT / with a lawyer
- Whether they'd use a tool like this before signing
- What would stop them trusting it

## Signal grading
### Strong
- They describe a real contract horror story
- They ask for access
- They say they'd use or pay for it

### Medium
- They acknowledge the pain but don't commit
- They compare it to current workaround behavior

### Weak
- Generic interest only
- No concrete pain story

| Date | Channel | Role | Pain | Interest | Trust concern | Follow-up |

## Conversion rule
A real conversation with concrete pain counts as progress even before a public page is live. Do not let missing hosting erase valid evidence.
