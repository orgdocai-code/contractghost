# ContractGhost MVP Spec

## One-line value prop
Upload a freelance contract and instantly see risky clauses, plain-English explanations, and safer rewrite suggestions before signing.

## Problem
Freelancers routinely sign contracts with hidden risk because legal review is expensive and slow. Common pain points include unlimited revisions, broad IP transfer, auto-renewal, one-sided termination, indemnity, and payment traps.

## Target user
- Freelancers
- Solo consultants
- Small creative studios
- Contractors using client-provided agreements

## MVP goal
Help a freelancer decide whether a contract is safe enough to sign or worth negotiating.

## What the MVP does
1. Accepts pasted text or uploaded contract text/PDF
2. Extracts clauses
3. Scores clauses by risk severity
4. Explains risk in plain English
5. Suggests a safer rewrite or negotiation point
6. Produces a concise summary: safe / caution / high risk

## What the MVP does NOT do
- Provide legal advice
- Replace a licensed lawyer
- Draft full custom contracts from scratch
- Support every jurisdiction at launch
- Store contracts long-term by default

## Core UX flow
1. User lands on page
2. Uploads contract or pastes text
3. Sees loading state
4. Gets a result page with:
   - overall risk summary
   - top flagged clauses
   - plain-English explanations
   - suggested edits
   - copy buttons for negotiation text
5. CTA to save/share/export result

## Initial risk categories
- Scope and revisions
- Payment terms
- IP ownership and licensing
- Termination and kill fees
- Liability and indemnity
- Exclusivity and non-compete
- Auto-renewal and notice periods
- Confidentiality asymmetry
- Dispute venue / governing law

## Trust positioning
- "Built for freelancers"
- "Plain English, not legal jargon"
- "Not legal advice"
- Encourage lawyer review for high-risk contracts

## Candidate pricing
