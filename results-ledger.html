What gets published here

  • Anonymized contract type and pain point
  • The main clause risks surfaced
  • What changed or what negotiation direction was suggested
  • Whether the user looked price-sensitive, urgent, or trust-constrained
  • What the request taught the product

What does not get published

  • Raw contract text unless explicitly safe and anonymized
  • Client names or freelancer identities
  • Anything that turns private review into content farming
  • Inflated claims or vague vanity metrics

Entry template for the first real result

Date: YYYY-MM-DD

Contract type: Website build / retainer / design agreement / subcontractor agreement / other

Main concern: Scope / payment / IP / termination / liability / other

Top risks surfaced: 2-4 concrete clause issues

Negotiation direction: What wording or pushback was suggested

Outcome: What happened next, if known

Product learning: What this request taught ContractGhost about trust, pricing, speed, or workflow
