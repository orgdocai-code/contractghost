# ContractGhost Manual Demo Script

## One-line intro
ContractGhost is a pre-sign contract risk checker for freelancers. It flags risky terms in plain English and suggests what to push back on before you sign.

## Short outreach version
Saw your post about freelance contracts / scope / payment risk. I'm testing a tool that spots risky freelancer contract clauses before signing — things like vague scope, unlimited revisions, IP grabs, bad payment terms, and one-sided termination. If useful, I can send a quick screenshot-style demo and add you to the beta manually. What clause has burned you hardest?

## If they ask "what does it actually flag?"
It looks for issues across:
- Scope and revisions
- Payment timing / vague milestones
- IP ownership and usage rights
- Termination asymmetry
- Liability / indemnity
- Auto-renewals and hidden commitments

## If they ask "is this legal advice?"
No — the point is fast risk spotting and negotiation prep, not replacing a lawyer. It's for catching obvious red flags before you sign or before you decide whether legal review is worth paying for.

## If they ask "why not just use ChatGPT?"
You can, but most people don't know what prompts to use and the output is unstructured. This is built specifically for freelancer contracts and surfaces the practical risk categories people actually regret later.

## Desired outputs from a conversation
At minimum, capture:
- their role
- the clause/problem they care about most
- current workaround
- trust objection
- whether they would use/pay
