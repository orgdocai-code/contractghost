What contract clause has burned you hardest as a freelancer?

**Body:**
I’m testing a tool called **ContractGhost** that reviews freelancer contracts and flags risky clauses in plain English before signing.

The idea is to catch things like unlimited revisions, vague payment terms, IP transfer before payment, one-sided termination, and indemnity traps without needing a full lawyer review for every small deal.

Not trying to replace a lawyer — more like: show me the dangerous bits fast so I know what to push back on.

If you’ve been burned before, what was the clause?
