Lower-trust intake path

Don’t want to send the whole contract? Send just the 3 clauses that worry you.

ContractGhost does not need the full document to be useful on day one. If privacy is the blocker, paste only the clauses you are worried about — usually scope, payment, IP, termination, liability, or revisions.

This is the lightest-weight validation path: a fast founder-led read on the specific contract language making you uneasy, without asking you to hand over the whole agreement first.
