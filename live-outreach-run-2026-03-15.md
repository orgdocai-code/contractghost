## Offer framing
ContractGhost helps freelancers review client contracts before signing.
It flags scope creep, IP grabs, weak payment terms, one-sided termination, indemnity, liability, and renewal traps in plain English.
It is positioned as issue-spotting + negotiation prep, not legal advice.

## Core validation questions
1. What contract clause or term has burned you hardest as a freelancer?
2. Did you catch it before signing or only after?
3. Would you trust a tool to flag that before signing if it explained why it mattered?
4. Would you pay $19-$29/month for that kind of pre-sign contract check?
