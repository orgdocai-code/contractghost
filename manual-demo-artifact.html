Panel 3

Why this clause is risky

Example clause
“Contractor agrees to continue revisions until Client is satisfied.”
Plain-English explanation

This can become endless unpaid revision work because there is no revision limit and no clear acceptance rule.

Why it matters

A vague satisfaction clause lets the client keep requesting changes without triggering a new fee.

Suggested pushback

Ask to limit revisions to 2 rounds and define approval within a fixed number of business days.

Example rewrite
“Project fee includes up to 2 revision rounds. Additional revisions are billed separately. Work is deemed accepted if no consolidated revision request is received within 5 business days.”
